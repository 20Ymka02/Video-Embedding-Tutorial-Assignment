How to Embed a Youtube Video

Follow this step-by-step guide to add videos to your own website.

Step 1: Click the "Share" Button

Open the video you want to use on Youtube. Below the video player, locate and click the Share button

Step 2: Choose the "Embed" Option

A pop-up menu will appear with various sharing options. Click on the Embed icon (represented by two angle brackets)

Step 3: Copy the HTML Code

A panel will open showing an <iframe> code block. Click the Copy button in the bottom right corner to save the code to your clipboard.
